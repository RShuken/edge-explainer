Telegram Communication Strategy

Two groups. Project-based. Less is more for Dan. Emily filters and manages operations. Dan focuses on what matters.

Less is More for Dan
Dan thinks in projects, not activity types. His group is project-scoped. No operational noise. No urgent email alerts (Emily filters those). Just his briefing, his meetings, and his projects.
Emily Filters Everything
Urgent emails go to Emily first. She decides what actually reaches Dan. Scheduling, contacts, drafts, approvals — all in her group. She's the operational shield.
Project-Based Topics
Dan says "Hey Edge, make a new project for Ensuring Colorado's Innovation Future." Topic appears instantly. Each project is self-contained: tasks, PRs, updates, deploys. Easy to create, easy to find.
Dan Can Peek Anytime
Dan can join Emily's group whenever he wants to see operational details. He just doesn't have to. His group stays clean. Her group has the full picture.

How Information Flows

  →

  →
Emily sees it first in #emily-urgent
  →
Emily flags to Dan only if truly urgent
